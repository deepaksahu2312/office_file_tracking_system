Abstract

Application Tracking System

This project is developed to digitize the process of application and verification for college documents and other purpose and to provide a hectic free solution for the process of circulating new forms.In this system a new Staff member or a Student can register themselves and then login to use the system.Students can apply for any ongoing event and their Application automatically goes to the concerned authority who can redirect it to higher authorities and then they can approve it or reject it and the Applicants will be able to track their applications in real time.
